# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want participation in the Accessly project to be a respectful and harassment-free
experience for everyone, regardless of background or identity.

## Expected behaviour

- Be respectful, considerate and constructive.
- Welcome differing viewpoints and accept constructive feedback gracefully.
- Focus on what is best for the project and the community.

## Unacceptable behaviour

- Harassment, insults, or discriminatory comments.
- Personal or political attacks.
- Publishing others' private information without consent.
- Any conduct that would reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces — issues, pull requests, discussions
and any other project communication — and when an individual represents the project publicly.

## Enforcement

Violations may be reported to the maintainer, Mouhssine Lakhili. Reports will be reviewed
and handled confidentially. Maintainers may remove, edit or reject contributions that do not
align with this Code of Conduct, and may temporarily or permanently restrict participation
for harmful behaviour.
